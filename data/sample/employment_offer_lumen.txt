Dear Hiroshi,

We are pleased to offer you the position of Senior Staff Software Engineer with Lumen Imaging Corp. ("Lumen" or the "Company"). This letter sets out the terms of your employment if you accept this offer.

1. Position and Duties.
You will report to the Chief Technology Officer and will be based in our San Francisco office, with the option to work remotely up to two days per week. You will devote your full business time and best efforts to Lumen and will not engage in any other employment without the Company's prior written consent.

2. Start Date.
Your anticipated start date is May 18, 2026, contingent upon successful completion of background and reference checks and verification of your eligibility to work in the United States.

3. Base Salary.
Your annualized base salary will be two hundred forty-five thousand U.S. dollars ($245,000), paid in accordance with the Company's standard payroll practices (currently semi-monthly) and subject to applicable withholdings.

4. Annual Bonus.
You will be eligible to participate in the Company's annual performance bonus program with a target bonus of twenty percent (20%) of your base salary. The actual amount paid each year will be determined by the Company in its sole discretion based on individual and Company performance. To receive any annual bonus, you must be employed by the Company on the date the bonus is paid.

5. Equity.
Subject to approval by the Board of Directors, you will be granted an option to purchase 35,000 shares of Lumen common stock at the fair market value on the date of grant. The option will vest over four (4) years, with twenty-five percent (25%) vesting on the first anniversary of your start date and the remaining seventy-five percent (75%) vesting monthly thereafter, subject to your continued service.

6. Benefits.
You will be eligible to participate in the Company's standard employee benefits, including medical, dental, and vision insurance; a 401(k) plan with employer match up to 4% of base salary; and an annual paid time-off allowance of twenty (20) business days, in addition to Company-observed holidays.

7. At-Will Employment.
Your employment with the Company is "at-will," meaning that either you or the Company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause and with or without notice. Nothing in this letter alters the at-will nature of your employment.

8. Confidentiality and Proprietary Information Agreement.
As a condition of employment, you must sign the Company's standard Confidential Information and Invention Assignment Agreement on or before your first day of employment.

9. Restrictive Covenants.
During your employment and for a period of twelve (12) months thereafter, you shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit any employee of the Company to terminate his or her employment with the Company. You acknowledge that this restriction is reasonable and necessary to protect the Company's legitimate business interests.

10. Severance.
If the Company terminates your employment without "Cause" (as defined in the Company's standard severance policy) outside of a change-of-control window, you will be eligible to receive six (6) weeks of base salary, paid in accordance with the Company's normal payroll practices, subject to your execution of a release of claims in a form provided by the Company.

11. Background and Eligibility.
This offer is contingent on (a) satisfactory completion of standard background checks; (b) verification of your right to work in the United States in accordance with the Immigration Reform and Control Act of 1986; and (c) execution of all required new-hire documents.

12. Entire Agreement.
This letter, together with the Confidential Information and Invention Assignment Agreement, constitutes the entire agreement between you and the Company regarding the terms of your employment and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings, written or oral.

Please indicate your acceptance of this offer by signing below and returning a copy by April 29, 2026.
